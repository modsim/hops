Constructs the Adaptive Metropolis proposal mechanism (Haario et al. 2001) on polytope defined as Ax<b.

Parameters
A
b
currentState
stepSizeThe stepsize by which the trained Gaussian proposal distribution is scaled.
epsScaling factor of the maximum volume ellipsoid, which is added to the covariance to preserve (numerical) irreducibility.
warmUpNumber of warm up samples during which the maximum volume ellipsoid is used as covariance of the proposal distribution. After the warm up, the adaptive covariance is used.
